# Super-Store-Sales-Analysis ( Power-BI )
## Project Objective 
Based on the 4-year dataset to understand the sale market and grow more sales in next years.

## Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/myolwinhein-data/Super-Store-Sales-Analysis-Power-BI-/blob/main/SuperStore.csv">superstore data</a>

## Questions ( KPIs )
- Which products stand as top five over revenue?
- Comparing revenue and profit quarterly.
- Who are our top customers?
- Which state provide the most revenue?

## Process
- Apply the data transforming and data cleaning and correct data formats.
- Create star schema for the relationship and to get better dashboard.
- Use dax functions to calculate total sales and profits.
- Apply slicers and charts to get meanful insights.

## Key Insights
- Observed a steady Year-over-Year growth starting from 2017, with a consistent revenue peak in Quarter 4, indicating strong year-end seasonality.
- Technology ( category products from slicer ) emerges as the primary revenue driver, followed by Office Supplies, while Furniture shows the lowest sales volume among the three Categories.
- California emerged as the highest-performing state, contributing significantly to total revenue, which indicates a strong product-market fit in the region.

## Final Conclusion
Based on the 4-year sales data analysis, the business shows strong growth potential, particularly in the Technology sector within the California market. To maximize future ROI, it is recommended to focus on Q4 inventory readiness, reduce logistics costs in high-performing regions, and implement a targeted retention strategy for top-tier customers.
